Coca-Cola discloses the general ingredients on its product labels, as required by food and beverage regulations. The primary components include carbonated water, high fructose corn syrup or sucrose (sugar), caramel color, phosphoric acid, and natural flavors. The specific combination and proportions of these ingredients contribute to the distinctive taste of Coca-Cola.
